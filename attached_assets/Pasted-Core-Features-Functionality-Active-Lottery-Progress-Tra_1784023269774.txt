Core Features & Functionality
Active Lottery & Progress Tracking: Users can browse high-value raffle pools, viewing live countdown timers, active participant numbers, the total volume of tickets remaining, and a progress bar showing how close the lottery pool is to being filled (e.g., "3% filled").

Flexible Number Selection: When buying a ticket, players can manually select their target "Lucky Numbers" or use a "Quick Pick" feature to generate random ticket numbers instantly.

Manual Payment Verification (Ethiopian Network Integration): Since standard automated credit card networks are not widely used locally, the application relies on an offline bank transfer flow. It provides instructions and accounts for Telebirr and the Commercial Bank of Ethiopia (CBE). The user completes the transfer externally and uploads an image/receipt of their payment to the platform.

Real-time Processing Feedback: Upon clicking "Submit Order," the application provides immediate feedback, giving the user a unique Order Number (e.g., #151) and setting expectations by stating that verification can take up to 24 hours.

Verification Tracking Dashboard: Users can input their phone number in the "Tickets" tab to check the real-time status of their entries (e.g., transitioning from "Waiting for admin payment approval" to an active ticket status).

1. The Discovery & Customization Phase
Home Screen / Detail Views: The user explores available lotteries, checks the countdown clock, and hits "Buy Ticket."

Raffle Setup: On the vehicle details modal, the user adjusts their ticket quantity (e.g., "1 ticket x 3,000 Birr") and selects their exact ticket number numbers.

2. The 3-Step Purchase Flow
Step 1 (Details): The user inputs their full name and their phone number with the Ethiopian prefix (+251).

Step 2 (Transfer & Proof): The user is shown banking destination information (Telebirr or CBE). After making an external transfer, they upload a screenshot of their transaction receipt and input their sender bank account.

Step 3 (Review): A detailed checkout invoice page summarizes the selected vehicle, ticket price, personal info, selected banking platform, and the uploaded receipt image.

3. Submission & Order Receipt (The New Screen)
Purchase Submitted Confirmation: Once submitted, a green confirmation screen pops up showing their unique Order ID. It informs the user that manual verification is underway and can take up to 24 hours to complete.

4. Tracking & Management
Tickets Dashboard: The user exits the checkout and navigates to the "Tickets" section of the app. By typing in their registered mobile number, they can track their pending entries and confirm when the platform admin has officially approved their transfer.
