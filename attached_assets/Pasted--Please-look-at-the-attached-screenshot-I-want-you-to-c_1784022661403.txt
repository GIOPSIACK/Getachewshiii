📱 Web Application Profile: "Gech EV Makina Ekub"

This application is a highly localized, digital raffle and crowd-funded lottery platform tailored specifically for the Ethiopian market. It modernizes the cultural concept of an "Ekub" (a traditional rotating savings and pooling association) by leveraging it to raffle off high-value assets—specifically modern electric vehicles, like the BYD Yuan UP.

The Core Problem It Solves:

In regions where purchasing a new vehicle outright is financially out of reach for most, and where seamless, automated API-based payment gateways are still evolving, this app provides a structured, trust-based, and accessible solution:

Crowd-funded asset acquisition: Users pool relatively small amounts (e.g., 3,000 Birr) to secure a raffle slot.

Manual verification bridge: It elegantly solves the lack of deep payment gateway integration by using a guided step-by-step workflow where users deposit funds via local services like Telebirr or the Commercial Bank of Ethiopia (CBE) and upload their transaction receipts directly into the app.
